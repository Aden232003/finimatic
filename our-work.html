Automated Short-Form Content Strategy

Jason & Michael Pizzino trading education transformation

440K YouTube Shorts 100K+ Instagram Reels
Statistic image generated by Finimatic team for illustration purposes due to restricted access to official Creator Studio analytics.

Challenge

The client had high-quality, long-form content but struggled to curate and convert it into engaging short-form videos for social platforms. Identifying the most valuable moments, editing for high retention, and integrating clear product calls-to-action (CTAs) was a major challenge.

Solution

  • Developed an automated short-form content strategy using raw long-form videos as source material
  • Curated, clipped, and produced engaging shorts by focusing on high-retention segments and integrating on-screen trading indicators and creator presence
  • Personalized edits for each platform, integrating CTAs for digital products directly within the videos
  • Created a dedicated SaaS product explainer video for a trading indicator tool, leading to successful product conversions
  • Curated and edited podcast video clips into highly engaging shorts for YouTube and Instagram, boosting overall retention and engagement
  • Also delivered long-form content edits (70,000+ views) with improved engagement metrics (likes, comments)

Results

  • ~500,000 total views across platforms (Shorts & Reels)
  • Significant boost in digital product sales and on-platform engagement
  • Notable increase in likes, comments, and channel visibility
  • Improved presence in key markets for the trading brand
